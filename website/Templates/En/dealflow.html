Bonsai Capital conducts active search per pre-defined criteria set together with the investor at given market segments. Bonsai Capital covers many market segments to include but not limited to: Security (HLS, Cyber), Clean-tech, Renewable energy, Water, Agriculture technology, Biotech, Healthcare (Medical devices, Pharma), Communication, Enterprise software, Internet, Mobile, Nano tech.

A thorough screening process is performed on every opportunity before brought up to investors for review and go/no-go decision on process continuation. The screening may include market and risk assessment, Initial review of financials, products, team, and valuation.
