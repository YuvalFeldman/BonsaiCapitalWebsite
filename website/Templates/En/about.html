About Us

Bonsai Capital's investment banking services are centered on international equity and technology made in Israel. Among our services:

  • Investment banking service – active search, deal-flow development, and investment process mediation for Israeli based technology serving foreign investment firms.
  • Corporate Funding – serve local Israeli companies in search for local/international funds directed at growing their business.
  • M&A – mergers and acquisitions both locally and internationally.
  • International business development – channel distribution development in international markets serving Israeli companies.

The company is led by two experienced managing partners:

  • Uri Feldman, a high tech veteran with over 20 years in operational leading roles, entrepreneur, with vast experience in M&A and fund raising.
  • Eran Ayzik, with over 20 years' experience in investment companies and high-tech management, former. A former venture capital funds partner with more than 100 investments

Bonsai Capital's strength lies in its leader's experience to identify and screen deal-flow for investment purposes and on its proven ability to make the right connections between investors/buyers and companies/sellers.

The firm has a working affiliation with Sunbelt Network, the world's largest M&A firm for SMB's with around 300 offices in all five continents and 3000 partners serving their local markets.
